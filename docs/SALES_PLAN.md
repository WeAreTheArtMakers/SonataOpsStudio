# Sales Plan

## ICP (Ideal Customer Profile)
- COO / VP Ops
- CIO / Head of Platform
- Head of Data / Analytics
- Director of Incident Management

## Pain Points
- Dashboard fatigue: too many panels, low actionability
- Incident fatigue: weak context and noisy handoffs
- Executive context gap: late, manual reporting cycles

## Unique Value
- Audio layer for anomaly salience during multitasking
- Grounded explanations with citations from internal docs
- Automated C-level brief generation with workflows

## Packaged Offers
### Pilot (2 weeks)
- 1 KPI domain
- up to 10 internal docs
- 1 executive brief template
- 1 automation flow

### Standard (6 weeks)
- up to 5 KPI domains
- full RAG corpus setup
- 3 automation flows
- evaluation kit and prompt governance setup

### Enterprise (12 weeks)
- SSO/RBAC integration
- governance controls and audit depth
- multi-tenant and environment promotion
- SLA package and managed support

## Sales Motion
1. Discovery call: identify one noisy KPI domain
2. Value workshop: map current incident/brief cycle costs
3. Pilot deployment in sandbox
4. Week-2 executive playback with quantified ROI
5. Expansion roadmap to additional domains and tenants

## Competitive Landscape
- **Not just dashboards**: adds sonification channel for cognitive load reduction.
- **Not just chatbots**: answers are grounded with citations + eval tracking.
- **Not generic workflow tools**: includes domain-ready anomaly and brief pipelines.
- **Not raw APM**: executive narrative layer and governance-first PromptOps.
